Why I Love Building Legos

The process of following instructions, piecing together tiny bricks, and seeing a creation come together was incredibly satisfying.

Over the years, my love for Legos has only grown. I enjoy the challenge of tackling complex sets, the creativity of designing my own builds, and the sense of accomplishment when a project is complete.
